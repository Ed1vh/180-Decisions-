180 Decisions — A Type 1 Diabetes Empathy Simulator
MindHack 2026 · Carleton University · Social Neuropsychology + HCI
A React application that interrupts your day with the decisions a person with Type 1 Diabetes faces every day. CGM-accurate alerts, clinical insulin calculator, and a long-term health consequence timeline grounded in DCCT/EDIC trial data.

To run: Paste 180-decisions-v7.jsx into a new React sandbox at codesandbox.io

Research sources: Stanford (180 decisions/day) · Walsh & Roberts 1994 (ICR/ISF formulae) · DCCT/EDIC Research Group, NEJM 1993/2000, Diabetes Care 2014 · Rustad & Musselman, J. Neuropsychiatry 2010 · The Lancet stigma consensus
